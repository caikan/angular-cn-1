Interpolation refers to embedding expressions into marked up text. By default, interpolation uses the double curly braces `{{` and `}}` as delimiters.

插值是指将表达式嵌入到被标记的文本中。默认情况下，插值使用双花括号 `{{` 和 `}}` 作为定界符。
